Mr. John is a delivery driver for a local restaurant. While on a delivery, he was hit by Miss. Marie, who was texting and driving at the time. As a result of the accident, Mr. John's car was badly damaged and he was unable to work for several weeks due to injuries sustained in the crash. Miss. Marie initially admitted her fault and promised to pay for the damages, but has since gone back on her word and is now refusing to pay. Mr. John is now unable to work and is struggling to pay his medical bills and make ends meet.
